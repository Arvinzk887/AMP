WHAT IS THE TRACK RECORD*:

I have several “All-Market Profit” type strategies. However, one of my flagship strategies has been backtested and forward- tested to roughly make twice the market returns of the S&P 500 with approximately one-third of the risk.

Since 2015, this strategy’s Compound Annual Growth Rate has been 20 point nine nine percent, indicating substantial growth year over year, outpacing benchmarks such as the S&P 500, which had a CAGR of approximately ten percentin the same period.

This particular strategy has maintained a relatively stable equity curve, minimizing the severity and length of drawdowns even during challenging market conditions.
