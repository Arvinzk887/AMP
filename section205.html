The All-Market Profits Trading Framework relies on several premises:

  • Future price movements cannot be accurately predicted, but volatility tends to revert to the mean.
  • The selling of options provides a statistically proven edge.
  • Controlling higher-order Greeks is the key to weathering all markets.

We will explore these premises throughout this book and detail why accounting for them can result in a truly non-directional trading strategy with the ability to weather all market environments.
