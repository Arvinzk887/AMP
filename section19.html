About The Author

Karl “The Transparent Trader” Domm is a veteran options trader with over three decades of experience in the markets. After figuring out how to trade for a living, Karl made it his mission to help other “battle-scarred” traders navigate the complexities of the market through his unique, non-directional, rules-based options strategies, which have helped countless traders succeed.

In addition to being the President of Clovis Wealth Partners and a licensed financial adviser, Karl is the creator of the YouTube channel, “Real P&L,” where he exposes fake trading instructors and openly shares the profit-and-loss statements of his trading strategies. Despite facing lawsuits and threats from other financial educators, Karl remains committed to helping traders make informed decisions by bringing transparency to the forefront of the trading education industry.

When he’s not trading or educating others, Karl enjoys spending time with his family and giving back to his community. He believes that success in trading should not come at the cost of personal relationships and that a well-balanced life is the key to long-term happiness and fulfillment.
